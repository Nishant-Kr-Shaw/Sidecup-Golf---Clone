ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

Please note: we are a cashless venue. The range closes at 10pm with last balls at 9pm.

TOP TRACER RANGE

Our 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range is powered by Toptracer Range technology. This allows you to track your shots, play games and even play virtual golf on some of the world's most famous courses.

GOLF LESSONS

Our team of PGA Professionals offer a range of coaching options for golfers of all abilities. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced player, we have a lesson package to suit you.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Our two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses are perfect for a fun day out with family and friends. No need to book, just turn up and play!
